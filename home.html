About

At Isa-Una, we ensure to only use quality ingredients and sell our creations at a fair price. We offer bold aromatic coffee and meals to satisfy your palate.

For espressos we carry the brand Cafe Moak and Lavazza from Italy. On the other hand, we brew coffee using beans from Indonesia mixed with Philippines.

Our food is inspired by local and international flavors. We're all about good food, using real ingredients, all natural, no preservatives, made with lots of love!
